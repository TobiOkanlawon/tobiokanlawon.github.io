# Live, Laugh, Love.

There's a secret   
Whispered by the sages   
Protected through the ages   
And it is this   

The heart yearns to love   
The mind yearns to know   
The arms yearn to hold   
The spirit forever knows   

Much of love   
Much of life   
Is not in the path finding,   
But in the path clearing.   


Us, babes, dark veils our shawls,   
They prevent and call it protection,   
Restrict and call it reception,   
Lie to us and call it narration   

Clear thine path, young sage,   
Yours, too, is the divine
